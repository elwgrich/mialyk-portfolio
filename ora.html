ORA ECOSYSTEM WEBSITE
Brand overview / interface direction / ecosystem framing
ORA is an AI / blockchain infrastructure project spanning multiple initiatives, products, and technical layers.
My role focused on bringing clarity to that complexity - not by reducing its ambition, but by giving it a stronger narrative, sharper structure, and more coherent visual direction.
The work extended across interface thinking, ecosystem communication, and broader creative output, shaping how ORA could be seen, understood, and experienced.

A large part of the work was not only visual execution,
but building a clearer logic for
how the ecosystem should be understood

01

Understand the system

I started by understanding the logic behind each initiative
not just the interface layer, but the protocol thinking underneath.

02

Translate complexity

I turned abstract research and technical concepts into clearer product and communication structures.

03

Build hierarchy across products

I created a more coherent information architecture
across DSC, IMO Launchpad, and AI Settlement.

04

Shape trust through design

I explored visual directions that felt precise, calm,
and system-led rather than overly promotional.

05

Align teams through design

Leading design acted as a bridge across
research, engineering, and go-to-market perspectives.

Offline Presence

Beyond
screens

Beyond the screen, I led the design team in extending ORA's visual direction into offline and ecosystem-facing contexts. The work focused on how ORA could show up in real-world settings with the same clarity, atmosphere, and cohesion, carrying the project's tone beyond interface design into broader community-facing moments.
